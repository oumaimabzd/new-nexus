Teacher School Security Course

Welcome to the Teacher School Security Course!

Safer schools start with prepared teachers. School safety is a shared responsibility, and teachers play a key role in prevention, early intervention, and effective response.

This course helps you recognize risks early, act confidently, and protect students and colleagues before, during, and after incidents.

How the course works

10 micro-lessons
Up to 25 minutes each, ~5 hours total. Five short segments per lesson (3–8 min each).
At your own pace
No need to finish in one sitting. Complete at your own pace.
Sequential learning
The lessons are designed to be completed in sequence, with each lesson building on the previous one.

You move from core principles, legal requirements and today's threats to practical prevention, response, and recovery skills.

What you will learn

You will gain the confidence to:

Understand school security principles
Recognize risks and warning signs
Apply laws, policies, and procedures
Address extremism, gangs, violence, and digital harm
Support positive behaviour and intervene early
Respond during emergencies
Help your school recover after crises

All content is practical and designed for everyday school life.

Lessons

Learn as you ask

School Nexus includes an AI assistant that answers security questions and recommends relevant lessons. You can explore topics when they arise and follow suggested content outside the normal sequence for deeper, just-in-time learning.

From knowledge to action

This micro-learning session combines several formats to support different learning styles and keep the content practical and engaging:

  • Video – introduces key concepts and real-world examples
  • Factsheet – provides clear guidance and actionable strategies
  • Interactive exercise – lets you practice the learned concepts and decision-making in realistic scenarios
  • Call to Action – helps you apply the learning immediately in your classroom
  • Questionnaire – reinforces understanding and reflection

Built on trusted expertise and evidence

The course is based on research, national guidance, and real-world experience. Content draws on leading Swedish authorities including Brå, Skolverket, CVE, and the Swedish Police Authority.

Security expertise is led by Marko Cabric, an experienced international security professional with direct school experience. The learning design is developed by Fyrtorn, specialists in pedagogy and instructional design.

Training is:

  • Evidence-based
  • Legally aligned
  • Practical
  • Designed for busy educators
  • Optimized for short, high-impact learning

Your role matters

School safety is built through everyday awareness and action. By completing this course, you become better prepared to prevent incidents and respond when they occur.
