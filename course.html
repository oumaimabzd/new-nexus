Teacher School Security Course

Welcome to the Course!

Safer schools start with prepared teachers. School safety is a shared responsibility, and teachers play a vital role in prevention, early intervention, and effective response.

You don't need a security background. This course equips you with clear, practical skills to spot concerns early, respond with confidence, and support your school community in any situation.

Evidence-based Legally aligned Practical Designed for busy educators Short, high-impact learning
Course Structure

How the Course Works

The course includes 10 practical micro-learning lessons, each 15–25 minutes long. Lessons are designed to be completed in sequence, with each one building on the previous lesson.

A gamified progress bar tracks your advancement and keeps you motivated as you move forward.

You'll progress from core principles, legal requirements, and today's threats to practical prevention, response, and recovery skills:

  1. 01 Foundations of School Security
  2. 02 Risks and Threat Awareness
  3. 03 Legal Framework and Responsibilities
  4. 04 Extremism
  5. 05 Gang Criminality
  6. 06 Deadly Violence
  7. 07 Digital Risks
  8. 08 Behaviour Management & Early Intervention
  9. 09 Incident & Emergency Management
  10. 10 Crisis Recovery

Learning Outcomes

What Will You Gain?

By completing the course, you will:

Build a strong foundation in school security principles and understand your role in creating a safer learning environment

Identify risks and warning signs early — from behavioural changes and online threats to signs of radicalisation and criminal exploitation

Navigate the legal framework with confidence — knowing your rights, responsibilities, and how to apply school policies in real situations

Recognise and respond to serious threats, including extremism, gang criminality, deadly violence, and digital harm

Promote positive behaviour and use early intervention strategies to prevent conflicts from escalating

Act decisively in emergencies using proven protocols to protect students and colleagues during critical incidents

Support recovery after a crisis — helping students, staff, and your school community heal and return to stability
